WALLICH RESIDENCE

The Tallest Residence in Singapore

Wallich Residence is a luxury private residence that occupies the 39th to 64th floors of Tanjong Pagar Centre and sits right on top of Tanjong Pagar MRT Station. Starting at 180m and soar to 290m above sea level, Wallich Residence is Singapore's tallest residential development.

 

Wallich Residence houses 181 luxury residences, consisting of one bedroom to four bedroom units, one Penthouses and one Super Penthouse. Each home is impeccably finished with top-notch materials and fittings. Residents will also benefit from access to Sofitel Singapore City Centre’s facilities and services as the 5-star hotel is situated in the same premises. The development is also integrated with the Tanjong Pagar MRT Station.
